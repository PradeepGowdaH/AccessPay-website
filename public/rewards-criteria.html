Reward Criteria

At AccessPay, we believe in appreciating our users for their financial activities. Our reward program is designed to give back to our community and encourage responsible financial behavior. Here's how you can earn rewards:

  1. Eligibility Criteria:
      - Users must have a registered account on our platform.
  2. Reward Calculation:
      - Users will earn a reward equivalent to 0.01% of their eligible spending.
  3. Reward Redemption:
      - Accrued rewards can be redeemed in the form of crypto tokens.
  4. How it Works:
      - For every eligible transaction within the specified spending range, users will automatically accumulate rewards.
      - Rewards will be credited to the user's account based on the 0.01% calculation.
      - Users can view their accrued rewards in their account dashboard.
  5. Terms and Conditions:
      - Rewards are calculated based on the transaction amount, excluding taxes, fees, and other non-eligible expenses.
      - Rewards are credited to the user's account after the completion of the eligible transaction.
      - Rewards have no cash value and can only be redeemed within our platform.
  6. Updates and Changes:
      - AccessPay reserves the right to modify, suspend, or terminate the reward program at any time without prior notice.
      - Any changes to the reward criteria will be communicated to users through our platform or other designated channels.
  7. Contact Us:
      - If you have any questions or concerns about our reward program, feel free to reach out to our support team at accesspay2024@gmail.com.

Thank you for being a valued member of AccessPay. Start earning rewards today by making eligible transactions within the specified spending range!
